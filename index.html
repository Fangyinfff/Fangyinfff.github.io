Yin Fang, Ph.D.

2024 Zhejiang University Ph.D.

2024 NIH Postdoc

I am a postdoctoral fellow at the National Library of Medicine, NIH, working with Dr. Zhiyong Lu. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Zhejiang University in June 2024, advised by Professors Huajun Chen, Ningyu Zhang, and Xiaohui Fan.

My research focuses on biomedical AI, especially large language models for biomedical reasoning, clinical trial matching, and single-cell analysis. I am currently leading TrialGPT, NIH/NLM’s AI-powered system for patient-to-clinical-trial matching.
